My name is Jen McPhail. In April 2016 I decided I was going to learn how to code.


I had been obsessed with internet culture since I was old enough to figure out how to dial up the modem on our Gateway PC and click on the Internet Explorer icon. I had also been toying with HTML, CSS, and JS from the time I was a preteen (s/o to my totally dope MySpace profile circa 2006.), but somewhere along the way, I had convinced myself that I wasn't good enough at math to become a real programmer.


Fast forward about ten years, one failed attempt at a drama degree, one successful attempt at a public relations degree and several digital marketing jobs later, I realized I wasn't really doing what I loved. So I took the plunge, headed to a coding bootcamp, and came out the other side a bonafide web developer.


This website is a collection of all the dev work I'm most proud of. Please take a look around. If you like what you see, get in touch by following me on social media, or reaching out via the contact page.
